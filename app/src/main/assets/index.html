The Landis Taipei is located at the very heart of Taipei’s vibrant district and is known for its long history as well as the hosting of numerous celebrities.<\/p>\r\n

Decorated in a French Art Deco style, the 209 newly renovated and tastefully decorated guestrooms and suites are fully equipped with the finest amenities to ensure a relaxing stay.<\/p>\r\n

Revel in any of 3 distinctive restaurants ranging from Tien Hsiang Lo, serving traditional Chinese Hangzhou gourmet, to Paris 1930, the most legendary French Restaurant in town and La Brasserie. Freshly made baked goods and pastries are available daily from Liz Gastronomie and all day in-room dining is also offered.
Other features include a 24-hour business center and a fitness center on the rooftop with natural light. Visitors can escape the bustle and be rejuvenated at Jurlique DAY SPA, the world-renowned Australian brand’s first hotel spa in Taiwan.<\/p>\r\n

The Landis Taipei enjoys an unrivaled location near the airport, as well as the city’s busy financial center. A mere 3-5minute walking distance from hotel to MRT station, providing easy access to major tourism spots, night markets and shopping districts.<\/p>\r\n
